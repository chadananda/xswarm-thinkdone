
## Part 2: Article Types

### Type A: Product Deep-Dive (60+ articles)
**Template:** History & founders → Philosophy & approach → Key features (with screenshots) → Strengths (genuine praise) → Limitations (honest, not harsh) → Who it was perfect for → How AI changes the equation → ThinkDone comparison → CTA

**Length:** 2,500-4,000 words
**SEO target:** "[App name] review", "[App name] pros and cons", "is [App name] still good"

### Type B: Feature Roundup (30+ articles)
**Template:** Define the feature/need → Why it matters → Top 8-12 apps ranked for this feature → ThinkDone's approach → Verdict
**Length:** 3,000-5,000 words
**SEO target:** "best app for [feature]", "top [feature] apps", "[feature] comparison"

### Type C: Methodology Guide (20+ articles)
**Template:** History & origin → Core principles → Step-by-step implementation → Tools that support it → Common mistakes → How AI enhances it → ThinkDone + [methodology]
**Length:** 3,000-6,000 words
**SEO target:** "[methodology] guide", "how to use [methodology]", "[methodology] for beginners"

### Type D: Head-to-Head Comparison (40+ articles)
**Template:** Quick verdict → Feature comparison table → Deep comparison by category → Use case recommendations → Where ThinkDone fits
**Length:** 2,000-3,500 words
**SEO target:** "[App A] vs [App B]", "[App A] or [App B]", "switch from [App A] to [App B]"

### Type E: Problem/Solution Article (25+ articles)
**Template:** Describe the pain point → Why it happens (psychology/workflow) → Traditional solutions → Why they fall short → The AI-first approach → How ThinkDone solves it
**Length:** 1,500-3,000 words
**SEO target:** "how to [solve problem]", "why can't I [problem]", "[problem] productivity"

### Type F: Listicle/Resource (15+ articles)
**Template:** Curated list with brief commentary on each item → ThinkDone mention in appropriate position
**Length:** 2,000-4,000 words
**SEO target:** "best [category] apps [year]", "top [number] [category] tools"

### Type G: Thought Leadership Essay (10+ articles)
**Template:** Big-picture essay on the future of productivity, AI's role, the death of manual planning, etc.
**Length:** 2,000-4,000 words
**SEO target:** Long-tail philosophical queries, social sharing, backlink generation

### Type H: How-To Cookbook (200+ articles)
**Template:** Name the real-world chaos scenario → Show the mess (relatable, no judgment) → Walk through the ThinkDone approach step-by-step with screenshots → Show the clean result → "Try it yourself" CTA
**Length:** 1,200-2,500 words
**Tone:** Warm, practical, zero jargon. Like a friend showing you their system over coffee. Each article solves ONE specific problem completely.
**SEO target:** "how to [organize X]", "how to manage [specific scenario]", "[scenario] productivity", "organize [messy situation]"

**What makes these different from Problem/Solution (Type E):** Type E articles are philosophical — they explain WHY you're stuck. Type H articles are tactical — they show you exactly HOW to fix it, step by step, with ThinkDone as the tool. Type E links to Type H. Type H is the conversion engine.
